MARDIV CAKES AND CATERING SERVICES

Located in the heart of Igabi, Kaduna, MARDIV CAKES AND CATERING SERVICES is your premier destination for custom-made cakes and comprehensive catering solutions. We specialize in creating memorable culinary experiences for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and all special occasions.

Our expert bakers and caterers combine traditional Nigerian flavors with modern techniques to deliver products that not only look stunning but taste absolutely divine. Every cake is baked fresh to order, and every catering menu is tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

With years of experience serving the Kaduna community, we pride ourselves on quality ingredients, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. Whether you need a simple birthday cake or full-service catering for 500 guests, MARDIV is here to make your event unforgettable.

What We Offer

Custom Cakes

Wedding cakes, birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, and celebration cakes designed to your exact specifications. Choose from vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, fruit cake, and more. Custom designs, fondant decorations, and edible prints available.

Catering Services

Full-service catering for weddings, corporate events, parties, and social gatherings. Nigerian cuisine, continental dishes, small chops, buffet setups, and service staff available. Customizable menus to suit your budget and guest count.

Small Chops & Pastries

Perfect for meetings, cocktails, and intimate gatherings. Includes puff puff, samosa, spring rolls, chicken wings, mini burgers, fish rolls, and assorted pastries. Fresh, hot, and delivered on time.

Corporate Catering

Professional catering solutions for conferences, seminars, training sessions, and office events. Reliable service, quality food, and timely delivery guaranteed.

Event Planning Support

We work with event planners and venues across Kaduna to ensure seamless coordination. Consultation services available to help you plan the perfect menu.
